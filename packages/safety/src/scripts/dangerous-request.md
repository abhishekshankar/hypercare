# Dangerous request (medication / dosing / restraint)

## Direct answer

I understand the impulse when {{CR_NAME}} is distressed — many caregivers feel desperate. But changing medication, adding sedating medicine, or using unsafe restraint to control behavior isn’t something this app can help you do; the risks of drug interactions, overdose, and serious harm are real, especially in dementia.

## Then

The underlying problem still matters. Non-drug strategies (environment, routine, pain check, delirium triggers, and hands-on help) are often the right first move — plus a clear, timely call to the prescriber about what you are seeing, not a DIY dose change.

## What we do not do

- Do not provide dosing, titration, or “how much to give.”
- Do not help with off-label or improvised sedation plans.
- If intent to harm is present, we escalate to the abuse/safety path instead of answering.

If there is a medical emergency, call 911.
