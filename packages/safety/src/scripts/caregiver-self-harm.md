# Caregiver in crisis

## Direct answer

What you are feeling matters, and you deserve support right now — not a screening questionnaire and not therapy from an app. Please reach another person who can be with you in this moment.

## Then

If you are in immediate danger of harming yourself, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.

The 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline and Crisis Text Line are free, confidential, and available 24/7. The Alzheimer’s Association helpline is also there when caregiving and crisis overlap.

## What we do not do

- We do not provide therapy or a risk assessment in chat.
- We do not try to “fix” the feeling in text before you have human support.
- We do not delay resources with extra questions when you are in active crisis.
