## Direct answer (self_harm_cr)

If you are afraid {{CR_NAME}} may hurt themselves or has talked about not wanting to live, treat this as urgent. Please call 911 if there is any immediate risk. You can also reach the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline for guidance; the Alzheimer’s helpline is available 24/7 for caregiving and safety in the same call.

## Then (self_harm_cr)

You are not overreacting. If you can, stay with {{CR_NAME}} or make sure they are not alone with means of harm. Follow what emergency dispatch tells you to do.

## What we do not do

- Do not diagnose.
- Do not downplay a missing-person or self-harm concern.
