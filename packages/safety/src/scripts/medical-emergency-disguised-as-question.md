# Medical emergency (even if the message sounds like a question)

## Direct answer

This sounds urgent. Please call 911 now. Short questions can still describe a medical emergency — especially with breathing, consciousness, choking, severe bleeding, stroke signs, or a bad fall.

## Then

If you are waiting for help: follow any instructions from the dispatcher, keep {{CR_NAME}} safe from further injury, and have a medication list and allergies ready. If the situation changes, call 911 again and say what changed.

## What we do not do

- Do not use this app to “confirm” that a dangerous symptom is normal.
- Do not discuss medication changes in place of emergency care.
- Do not delay calling when symptoms are red-flag.
